WOCY

Invention Application User Research UI/UX

OVERVIEW

Wocy consists of a hormone measuring device and an app that work together to track often invisible and complex hormonal changes and make them easier to understand. Users can monitor whether their values are within a normal range, receive helpful tips when irregularities occur, and seek medical advice in more serious cases.

PROCESS

01 Brainstorming

We began by brainstorming future-oriented ideas that could become realistic within the next 10-20 years. After exploring different fields, we focused on women’s health and the hormonal system.

02 Concept

Our concept supports women during menopause through a hormone measuring device, similar to a diabetes sensor. The collected data is visualized in an app, making hormonal changes easier to understand and manage.

03 Design

For the implementation, we started with wireframes to structure the key features and define the app layout. We then explored different visual design directions while also developing the design of the hormone measuring device.
